## 📋 Project Steps

1. Cleaned and standardized the dataset

## 📌 Key Insights

<details>
<summary>💰 Income Analysis</summary>

| Gender | Avg. Income (Buyers) |
|--------|----------------------|
| Male   | > $60,000            |
| Female | > $55,000            |

</details>

<details>
<summary>👥 Demographics</summary>

| Finding | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| Top age group | Middle-aged (31–54 years) |
| Top occupation | Professional |
| Top education | Bachelor's degree |
| Marital status | Married |

</details>

<details>
<summary>🌍 Market & Commute</summary>

| Category | Insight |
|----------|---------|
| Top region | North America |
| Common commute | 0–1 mile |

</details>

📍 Bikes were predominantly purchased for commutes with distances between 0 and 1 mile.

📍 The majority of bicycle purchases were made by individuals who hold professional occupations, or possess a Bachelor's degree in Education, or are married.

📍 North America had the highest number of bicycles sold, indicating a strong market demand for bicycles in the region.
